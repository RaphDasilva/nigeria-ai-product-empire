## Style Notes

- **Background:** Deep navy blue gradient — `#0A1628` (top) → `#1E3A5F` (bottom)
- **Primary Text (JAMB CBT 2025):** Metallic gold `#FFD700`, bold, large (title size), centered, subtle drop shadow
- **Secondary Text (Ultimate Practice Pack):** White `#FFFFFF`, semi-bold, medium size
- **Feature Text (500+ Questions...):** Light blue `#A8CFFF`, small weight, bottom strip
- **Badge:** Red `#E53E3E` ribbon in top-right corner, white text "NEW 2025"
- **Overlay Pattern:** Circuit board or dot grid — dark teal `#0D4F6B` at 20% opacity
- **3D Element:** Open PDF/tablet mockup showing exam Q&A interface
- **Photography Element:** Nigerian student silhouette (diverse, young, focused)
- **Mood:** Confident, premium, urgent, trustworthy — "this will help you pass"
- **Typography Style:** Modern sans-serif (like Inter, Nunito, or Poppins)
