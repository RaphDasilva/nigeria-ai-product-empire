## General Rules for Objection Handling

1. **Acknowledge first** — Never dismiss their concern
2. **Reframe, don't argue** — Show a different angle
3. **Keep it short** — 3–5 sentences max. Long replies feel like desperation.
4. **End with a question or CTA** — Keep the conversation going
5. **One follow-up only** — If they still say no after your response, wish them well and move on
6. **Never badmouth competitors** — Stay professional
